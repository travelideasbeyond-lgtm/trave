Azerbaijan

(36 reviews)

Azerbaijan Delight – 4N Baku + 2N Shahdag (Day Trip to Quba)

  • Day 1 – Arrive in Baku* Check in and relax. In the evening, walk along Baku Boulevard and enjoy the lighting show at Flame Towers.
  • Day 2 – Full Baku Tour* Explore the Old City, Carpet Museum, Nizami Street and the futuristic Heydar Aliyev Center. Optional: Enjoy a Caspian Sea boat ride.
  • Day 3 – Absheron Peninsula Highlights* Visit Fire Temple, Burning Mountain and the Bibi-Heybat Mosque. Later, explore the local bazaar for souvenirs and Azeri treats.
  • Day 4 – Transfer to Shahdag* Drive to Shahdag Mountain Resort. Check in, relax and enjoy views of snow-capped peaks or try some adventure sports.
  • Day 5 – Day Trip to Quba* Visit Quba’s charming town, Qecresh forest, and witness the famous Quba mosque and apple orchards. Back to Shahdag for a cozy mountain evening.
  • Day 6 – Departure* Head back to Baku or directly to the airport depending on flight timing.
